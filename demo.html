Bach: Invention No. 1
Mozart: Sonata K 330

Training and test data

The HMMs are trained and tested using different subsets of PIG Dataset. To get access to the dataset and see details of the data format, please visit the PIG Dataset webpage.

Visualizer

This visualizer can be used for observing and comparing the above estimation results as well as the fingerings in PIG Dataset. It works on browsers such as Google Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Microsoft Edge, etc.
